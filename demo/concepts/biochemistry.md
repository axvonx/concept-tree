# Biochemistry

Biochemistry studies chemical processes within and relating to living organisms. It bridges chemistry and biology, explaining life at the molecular level.

## Core Topics

- **Metabolism** — enzyme-catalyzed reactions producing or consuming energy (glycolysis, citric acid cycle, oxidative phosphorylation)
- **Proteins** — structure (primary → quaternary), folding, and function
- **Lipids** — membranes, signaling, energy storage
- **Carbohydrates** — energy currency and structural roles
- **Enzymes** — catalysts that lower activation energy; subject to allosteric regulation
