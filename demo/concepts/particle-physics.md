# Particle Physics

Particle physics studies the fundamental constituents of matter and the forces acting between them. The Standard Model describes all known elementary particles and three of the four fundamental forces.

## Elementary Particles

- **Quarks** — building blocks of protons and neutrons (6 flavors)
- **Leptons** — electrons, muons, taus, and neutrinos
- **Gauge bosons** — force carriers: photon (EM), W/Z bosons (weak), gluons (strong)
- **Higgs boson** — gives mass to other particles via the Higgs field
