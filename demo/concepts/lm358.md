# LM358 Dual Op-Amp

The LM358 is a general-purpose dual op-amp in a DIP-8 package, capable of single-supply operation from 3V to 32V. It's the classic beginner's op-amp: cheap, widely available, and forgiving. Its input common-mode range extends to the negative rail, making it well-suited to single-supply designs.

Limitations: 1MHz gain-bandwidth product, ~0.6V/µs slew rate, and significant input offset voltage. Not suitable for audio or high-frequency work — but perfect for comparators, low-frequency amplifiers, and sensor interfaces.

**Product:** LM358P (Texas Instruments, DIP-8)  
**Supply:** 3V – 32V single / ±1.5V – ±16V dual  
**GBW:** 1 MHz
