# Neuroscience

Neuroscience is the scientific study of the nervous system — the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves. It integrates biology, chemistry, physics, and psychology to understand how neural circuits give rise to behavior and cognition.

## Key Areas

- **Cellular neuroscience** — neurons, synapses, action potentials
- **Systems neuroscience** — sensory and motor circuits, perception
- **Cognitive neuroscience** — memory, attention, language, decision-making
- **Computational neuroscience** — mathematical models of neural function
