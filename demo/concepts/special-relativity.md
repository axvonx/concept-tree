# Special Relativity

Special relativity applies to all physical phenomena in the absence of gravity. It was published by Einstein in 1905.

Key postulates:
- The laws of physics are the same in all inertial frames of reference
- The speed of light in vacuum is the same for all observers

This leads to time dilation, length contraction, and the famous equation **E = mc²**.
