# AD9833 Waveform Generator

The AD9833 from Analog Devices is a programmable waveform generator that outputs sine, triangle, or square waves from 0Hz to 12.5MHz. It's controlled over SPI from any microcontroller and runs on 2.3V–5.5V. Frequency and phase are set in 28-bit registers, giving sub-Hz resolution at low frequencies.

Breakout boards pair the IC with a small output filter and SMA connector. Common uses: function generator, audio tone source, modulation carrier, clock source.

**Product:** AD9833 Programmable Waveform Generator Module  
**Frequency range:** 0 – 12.5 MHz  
**Output types:** sine, triangle, square  
**Interface:** SPI (FSYNC, SCLK, SDATA)
