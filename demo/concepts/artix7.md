# Digilent Nexys A7 (Xilinx Artix-7)

The Xilinx Artix-7 is an industry-standard FPGA used in communications, industrial control, and video processing. The **Digilent Nexys A7** development board makes it accessible for students and engineers with onboard USB-JTAG, 128MB DDR2, VGA, UART, and a full suite of switches, LEDs, and seven-segment displays.

Designed for Vivado, Xilinx's IDE. The Nexys A7 is the platform for many university digital design courses and is compatible with thousands of community projects.

**Product:** Digilent Nexys A7-100T FPGA Trainer Board  
**Device:** Xilinx XC7A100T-1CSG324C  
**Logic cells:** 101,440  
**Block RAM:** 4.86Mbit  
**DSP slices:** 240
