# Physics

Physics is the natural science that studies matter, its fundamental constituents, its motion and behavior through space and time, and the related entities of energy and force.

Key areas include mechanics, thermodynamics, electromagnetism, optics, and quantum physics. Modern physics has expanded to include nuclear physics, particle physics, and cosmology.
