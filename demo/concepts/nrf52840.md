# nRF52840 Development Kit

The Nordic nRF52840 is an ARM Cortex-M4 (64MHz) MCU with integrated Bluetooth 5, Thread, Zigbee, and 802.15.4 radio. It's the gold standard for low-power BLE devices: ultra-low sleep current (~1µA in System OFF), hardware-accelerated AES/ECC, USB Full Speed, and 1MB flash.

The **nRF52840 DK** (PCA10056) is Nordic's official development board with onboard J-Link debugger, 4 buttons, 4 LEDs, Arduino headers, and both SMA and PCB antenna options. It's the reference platform for testing Bluetooth, Thread, and Zigbee firmware.

**Product:** Nordic nRF52840 DK (PCA10056)  
**CPU:** ARM Cortex-M4F @ 64 MHz  
**Flash:** 1 MB  
**RAM:** 256 KB  
**Radio:** Bluetooth 5 / Thread / Zigbee / 802.15.4
