# Breadboard

A solderless breadboard lets you prototype circuits by pressing component leads and jumper wires into a grid of spring-loaded contacts. The rows are connected horizontally in groups of five, with long power rails running vertically along each side.

**Product:** BB830 Solderless Plug-in Breadboard  
**Tie points:** 830  
**Specs:** 6.5" × 2.125", 400mA max per rail  
**Buy:** [Adafruit](https://www.adafruit.com/product/239) · [Mouser](https://www.mouser.com/ProductDetail/BusBoard-Prototype-Systems/BB830)

The BB830 is a full-size board that accommodates DIP ICs straddling the center gap, leaving 5 contact holes on each side per pin — standard for almost every beginner tutorial.
