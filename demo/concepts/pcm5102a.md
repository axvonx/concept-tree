# PCM5102A Audio DAC

The PCM5102A is a 32-bit, 384kHz stereo audio DAC from Texas Instruments. It accepts I²S digital audio and outputs a clean analog signal through an integrated analog output stage — no external op-amp needed. This makes it ideal for high-quality audio projects driven by a Raspberry Pi, ESP32, or any I²S-capable microcontroller.

It runs off a single 3.3V supply and communicates over I²S. Breakout boards from GY-PCM5102 expose all the relevant pins and include decoupling for minimal noise.

**Product:** GY-PCM5102 I²S DAC Breakout  
**Resolution:** 32-bit  
**Sample rate:** up to 384kHz  
**SNR:** 112dB
