# Cosmology

Cosmology is the study of the origin, evolution, and ultimate fate of the universe as a whole. It combines general relativity, particle physics, and observational astronomy.

## Key Topics

- **Big Bang** — the universe began ~13.8 billion years ago from an extremely hot, dense state
- **Cosmic inflation** — exponential expansion in the first fraction of a second
- **Dark matter** — non-luminous matter inferred from gravitational effects (~27% of the universe)
- **Dark energy** — mysterious force driving accelerated expansion (~68% of the universe)
- **CMB** — cosmic microwave background radiation, the afterglow of the Big Bang
