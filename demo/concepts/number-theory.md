# Number Theory

Number theory studies integers and integer-valued functions. One of the oldest branches of mathematics, it has deep connections to cryptography, combinatorics, and algebraic geometry.

## Key Topics

- **Primes** — integers greater than 1 with no divisors other than 1 and themselves; infinitely many
- **Modular arithmetic** — arithmetic on remainders; foundation of RSA cryptography
- **Fermat's Last Theorem** — no solutions to xⁿ + yⁿ = zⁿ for n > 2 (proved 1995)
- **Goldbach's conjecture** — every even integer > 2 is the sum of two primes (unproven)
- **Riemann hypothesis** — about the distribution of primes; one of the Millennium Prize Problems
