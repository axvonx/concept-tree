# Genetics

Genetics is a branch of biology concerned with the study of genes, genetic variation, and heredity in organisms.

Gregor Mendel, a Moravian Augustinian friar working in the 19th century, is often considered the "father of genetics." His work with pea plants established many of the rules of heredity.
