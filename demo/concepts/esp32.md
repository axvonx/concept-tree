# ESP32 DevKit

The ESP32 from Espressif is a dual-core 32-bit LX6 MCU running at 240MHz with integrated Wi-Fi (802.11 b/g/n) and Bluetooth 4.2 (Classic + BLE). It packs 520KB SRAM, up to 16MB flash, 34 GPIO pins, 12-bit ADC, I²C, SPI, UART, I²S, and hardware-accelerated cryptography — all at ~$3.

The ESP32 DevKit breakout board adds USB-serial (CP2102), 3.3V LDO, and breaks out all pins on 0.1" headers. Program it with Arduino, ESP-IDF (native Espressif SDK), MicroPython, or CircuitPython.

**Product:** ESP32-WROOM-32 DevKit v1  
**CPU:** Dual-core Xtensa LX6 @ 240 MHz  
**RAM:** 520 KB SRAM  
**Radio:** Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n + Bluetooth 4.2 (Classic + BLE)  
**GPIO:** 34 pins
