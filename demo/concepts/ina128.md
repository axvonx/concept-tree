# INA128 Instrumentation Amplifier

The INA128 is a precision instrumentation amplifier designed to amplify small differential signals in the presence of large common-mode voltages — exactly what you need when reading thermocouples, strain gauges, or ECG electrodes.

Unlike a standard op-amp diff amp (which needs four matched resistors), the INA128 sets gain with a single external resistor: G = 1 + 50kΩ/Rg. Input impedance is 10GΩ. CMRR is 120dB minimum at unity gain.

**Product:** INA128PA (Texas Instruments, DIP-8)  
**Gain range:** 1 – 10,000 (via single Rg)  
**Input offset:** 25µV max  
**CMRR:** 120dB min
