# Molecular Biology

Molecular biology studies biological activity at the molecular level — especially the interactions between DNA, RNA, and proteins. It bridges biochemistry and genetics.

## Central Dogma

**DNA → RNA → Protein**

- **Replication** — DNA copies itself before cell division
- **Transcription** — DNA is read to produce messenger RNA
- **Translation** — ribosomes read mRNA to synthesize proteins

Gene expression is regulated by transcription factors, epigenetics, and small RNAs.
